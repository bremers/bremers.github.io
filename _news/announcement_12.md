My newest work on collaborative creative machines will be showcased at the ACM Symposium on Computational Fabrication 2023 at Cornell Tech, NYC. Intern Itay Grinberg did a great job modifying an AxiDraw pen plotter to allow for extra degrees of freedom, enabling interaction through dance movements. Come visit us on October 9th! As part of this work, I am also looking to talk to computational fabricators to learn about the tools and machines that are used.
