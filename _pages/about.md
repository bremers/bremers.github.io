I am currently a 3rd year PhD student in the Information Science department at Cornell University, based at Cornell Tech (NYC). My advisor is associate professor Wendy Ju in the Future Automation Research Lab.

I am a recipient of grants by the Finnish Center for AI and Cornell Backslash Art, and I was selected for the Oxford-Cambridge RisingWISE cohort of 2018. 

My research focuses on human-robot collaboration for physical manufacturing and fabrication. The methods that I use focus most on interaction design, video analysis, and interactive device prototyping. Ongoing and past projects include industry collaborations with Toyota Research Institute, Nissan Motors, Amada Co. Ltd. and Accenture Labs, as well as NSF-funded projects.

Prior to starting the PhD, I obtained a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University in 2018 (MSc), and a bachelor's degree in Industrial Design from Eindhoven University of Technology in 2016 (BSc). Prior to starting my PhD, I was employed as Human-Machine Interface Researcher at Jaguar Land Rover Automotive PLC, based at the National Automotive Innovation Centre in Coventry, United Kingdom. In addition, I have worked internationally as a designer and researcher for organizations in Amsterdam, Taipei and Los Altos (CA).

I advise students at the graduate and undergraduate level - contact myself or my advisor if you are interested in working with us.
