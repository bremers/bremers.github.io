I am a designer and researcher from The Netherlands. In my day job I design and develop interactive, artificially intelligent systems for use inside the car cabin. I obtained degrees in Industrial design from Eindhoven University of Technology, and Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University. On this website I showcase my public work. You can also find my CV, recent news, as well as links to my other social media accounts. This website is currently a work in progress - more materials will be added along the way.
