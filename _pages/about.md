I am a PhD student at Cornell Tech. My research interests focus around everyday interactions with intelligent cyber-physical systems, and their impacts on productivity and wellbeing. I obtained degrees in the Netherlands in Industrial Design from Eindhoven University of Technology, and Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University, and worked for a while in the British automotive industry. On this personal website I showcase my public work. You can also find my CV, recent news, as well as links to my other social media accounts. This website is currently a work in progress - more materials will be added along the way. The website works best on a pc or laptop.
