I am a designer and researcher from The Netherlands, residing in the United Kingdom. In my day to day work I design and develop interactive, artificially intelligent systems for use inside the car cabin. My wider interests focus around everyday interactions with intelligent cyber-physical systems, and their impacts on productivity and wellbeing. I obtained degrees in Industrial Design from Eindhoven University of Technology, and Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University, both in The Netherlands. On this personal website I showcase my public work. You can also find my CV, recent news, as well as links to my other social media accounts. This website is currently a work in progress - more materials will be added along the way. The website works best on a pc or laptop.
