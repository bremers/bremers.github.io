I conducted two perception experiments to understand depth perception around the perspective cue in a Head-Up Display, to inform engineering and design. This work was part of my master's thesis at Utrecht University, done in collaboration with Jaguar Land Rover and the University of Cambridge.

The research was covered by Forbes, Yahoo! News, and Jaguar Land Rover's media channels.

**Publication:**
- Alexandra W.D. Bremers et al. "Perception of Perspective in Augmented Reality Head-Up Displays". In: *International Journal of Human-Computer Studies* (2021). [doi:10.1016/j.ijhcs.2021.102693](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijhcs.2021.102693)
