Building on the BAD dataset work, this project explores anticipatory human reactions for outcome prediction in human-robot interaction. By analyzing bystander facial and body responses, we investigate whether human social cues can predict robot task success or failure before the outcome is known.

**Publications:**
- Maria Teresa Parreira, Sukruth Gowdru Lingaraju, Adolfo Ramirez-Aristizabal, Alexandra Bremers, et al. "'Bad Idea, Right?' Exploring Anticipatory Human Reactions for Outcome Prediction in HRI". In: *IEEE RO-MAN 2024*. [doi:10.1109/RO-MAN60168.2024.10731310](https://doi.org/10.1109/RO-MAN60168.2024.10731310)
- Maria Teresa Parreira et al. "'Why the face?': Exploring Robot Error Detection Using Instrumented Bystander Reactions". Under review, 2025.
