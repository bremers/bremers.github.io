During my internship at Next Nature Network, I was responsible for finalizing the Bistro In Vitro Ice Cart project, exhibited at Dutch Design Week 2015. This project promoted Meat The Future, featuring a fictional cookbook with recipes questioning the possibilities of cooking with lab-grown meat.

I developed graphical artefacts including booklets, an interactive presentation, and flyers, and operated the Bistro in Vitro Ice Cart during the exhibition.
