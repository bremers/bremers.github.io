Conversational user interfaces have potential in helping people with guidance during physical tasks. To investigate various forms of feedback, I worked with Accenture Labs to build a Wizard-of-Oz system with Raspberry Pi and cameras that tested different audio cues as feedback for performing assembly tasks.

The results were published at CUI 2024 and fed into further research at Accenture Labs.

**Publication:**
- Alexandra Bremers, Manaswi Saha, Adolfo Ramirez-Aristizabal. "Situated Conversational Agents for Task Guidance: A Preliminary User Study". In: *ACM CUI 2024*. [doi:10.1145/3640794.3665575](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3640794.3665575)
