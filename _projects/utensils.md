This project explored the expression of aesthetic qualities through visuals and materials. Starting from a collage around the words "fast," "intense," and "repulsive," the assignment was to develop a set of objects in which these aesthetic qualities were expressed physically.

The final objects were made from clay with a spray-painted finish, wood, and carved potato with alpaca wool. Completed during the second year of my B.Sc. in Industrial Design at TU Eindhoven.
