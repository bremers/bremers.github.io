During my Industrial Design degree, I spent time investigating unique experiences possible with light. This installation, based on retro-reflectivity, consisted of a dark room experienced in groups. One person would wear LED-implemented eyeglasses, which would enable them to see the art covering the walls.

As such, light becomes a private experience in a shared space.
